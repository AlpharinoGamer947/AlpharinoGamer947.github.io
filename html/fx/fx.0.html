#2 The Graveyard


          With a day like this, it can't get worse right? It did very quickly. After eating dinner, I got my hand healed by a healer. And my arm was back to normal. My friends: Gale, Storm, Flame, Frost, Aqua, and Flash called me so we could hangout and I silently left my house. We were wandering in the city because we were bored. My friend Gale , told us that we could go to The Midnight Restaurant to get a midnight snack.


          As we were walking to The Midnight Restaurant, we passed through an alleyway. Flash stopped us and told us that he saw a creepy green gooey light coming from a small brown rickety wooden door that withstood time. As we took a closer look, it looked like a wooden gate than a door. The door had a strange symbol and it looked like the letters f and x stuck together.


          We were very curious so we opened the gate and found a lot of vehicles that were rusted. It was like a graveyard for vehicles. The fog was mild with a tint of green. Storm pointed out that the fire that was present in the graveyard was green, including the moon. There were also a lot of dead trees.


#3 The Corroded Vehicle


          As we were wandering around the spooky graveyard, I found a truck, not just any truck, a normal truck with a flatbed trailer. Why was it not normal you ask? Because it was completely clean, like a new truck. The keys were also in the door lock on the driver's side.


          Frost and Flame were quarreling about whether to report this mysterious place to the city authorities. I understand that we should report a mysterious graveyard to the authorities but also I wanted it to be hidden. All the vehicles we saw had the exact same symbol we found on the wooden gate we entered through.


          Gale found a very peculiar vehicle that was close to the truck. It was very dirty and had a lot of rust. It was around the size of an SUV. It had spikes in the front of the body and they were green in color. The windscreen had the same color as the spikes.


          I was searching for a way to lift the vehicle onto the flatbed and was in the rear of it. There was a full sized turbo jet engine, a JET ENGINE! These are found in military jets and airplanes. According to Aqua, it's part car, tank, jet and racecar.
